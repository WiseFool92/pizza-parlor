Why Programming

To explain why I have chosen programming, we have to go back to the beginning. Growing up my uncle was heavily involved with technology. He exposed my cousin and I at an early age and taught us to use computers. Fast forward to highschool, I essentially lived in the networking room and began to cultivate my skills with database/network programming via Cisco modems. Fast forward to college, the only reason I didn't choose computer science as a major is that I felt self concious choosing the same major as 90% of my friends. Throughout my college years I have remained connected to tech and gravitated towards CS,ENG, and MED majors. I can now say that I am happy that I have finally begun to pursue a career in tech, where I have always belonged. It just took me a few years to rediscover where my passions lie.
